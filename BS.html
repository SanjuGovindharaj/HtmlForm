In this chapter, we will look at some HTML elements that will be styled a little bit differently by Bootstrap than browser defaults. Bootstrap's grid system is responsive, and the columns will re-arrange depending on the screen size: On a big screen it might look better with the content organized in three columns, but on a small screen it would be better if the content items were stacked on top of each other.
